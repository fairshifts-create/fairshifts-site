Introduction

FairShifts ("we", "us") provides done-for-you staff rostering services for care homes and security firms. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

What we collect

Your contact details (name, email, organisation) when you subscribe or message us.

What we collect

  • Staff first names or anonymised IDs
  • Roles
  • Teams
  • Contracted hours
  • Availability and leave dates
  • Your scheduling rules

What we never ask for

  • Staff surnames
  • Addresses
  • Dates of birth
  • ID numbers
  • Phone numbers
  • Health information
  • Bank details

We don't yet collect payments directly through this website — when online billing launches for our Pro and Business plans, it will go through Stripe's secure checkout, and card numbers will never touch our systems.

How we use it

Solely to build, check, and deliver your rosters, manage your subscription, and reply to you. We never sell data or use it for advertising.

Who we share it with

Only the services that run FairShifts: Formspree (intake forms), Google Drive (file storage), and Vercel (website hosting).

Once online billing launches, Stripe (payments) will be added to this list. No one else.

Data retention

We keep your roster files and intake data while you're a customer. If you cancel, everything is deleted within 90 days. Invoices are kept as long as tax law requires.

Your rights

Email us anytime for a copy of your data or to have it deleted; we respond within one month. You can also complain to the data protection authority in the United Arab Emirates.

Security

Two-factor authentication on our accounts and minimal data collection by design. Once online payments launch, they'll be handled by PCI-DSS certified Stripe.

Cookies & analytics

This website uses no tracking cookies and no advertising trackers.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to fairshifts@gmail.com.
